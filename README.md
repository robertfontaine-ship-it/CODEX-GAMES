# WRS Quest

**Flagship campaign:** **Corpocalypse: Climb the Corporate Ladder**

WRS Quest is a browser-first narrative arcade RPG that teaches Workplace Readiness Skills through workplace decisions, visible consequences, short job challenges, and promotion-based progression.

## Play now

### [▶ Play Floor 1 — Intern Orientation](https://raw.githack.com/robertfontaine-ship-it/CODEX-GAMES/main/preview/index.html)

### [🧪 Launch Floor 1 in Playtest Mode](https://raw.githack.com/robertfontaine-ship-it/CODEX-GAMES/main/preview/index.html?playtest)

These links open the committed, self-contained browser build. No download, extraction, account, or local installation is required.

Playtest Mode displays a yellow badge, records the session locally, and adds the Playtest Lab after the performance review. No data is transmitted automatically.

## Current milestone

Floor 1 — Intern Orientation is playable and ready for human playtesting.

The vertical slice includes:

- comic-style OmniCorp outbreak briefing
- four workplace encounters with consequences
- XP, Trust, Credits, and Time Bank progression
- Inbox Zero timed job challenge
- Elevator Escape promotion gate
- Workplace Readiness Skill tracking
- performance review and promotion result
- replayable alternate outcomes
- browser-local save and settings
- optional local-only playtest tracking and feedback export

## Product principle

This is **not** a quiz game with zombies. Workplace decisions are the gameplay. WRS standards operate as invisible infrastructure behind story consequences, allies, challenge modifiers, progression, and reporting.

## Floor 2 decision gate

Floor 2 does not begin until Floor 1 playtesting demonstrates:

- average fun rating of at least 4/5
- average clarity rating of at least 4/5
- most players notice that earlier choices affect later gameplay
- no repeated navigation blocker
- no repeated misunderstanding of promotion requirements
- typical completion time of 10–15 minutes
