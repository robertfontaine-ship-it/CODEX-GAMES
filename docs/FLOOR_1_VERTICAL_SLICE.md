# Floor 1 — Intern Orientation

## Player promise

A 10–15 minute first shift in which workplace decisions change the player's allies, resources, challenge timers, and promotion result.

## Playable sequence

1. **Opening briefing** — arrive at OmniCorp during a corporate zombie outbreak.
2. **The Vague Assignment** — communication, responsibility, and clarification.
3. **Confidential Data Exposed** — information security, integrity, and responsibility.
4. **Team Meltdown** — conflict resolution, teamwork, and time management.
5. **Password Required Immediately** — phishing detection and critical thinking.
6. **Inbox Zero** — timed sorting challenge with urgency, priority, customer service, safety, and security decisions.
7. **Elevator Escape** — promotion gate whose timer and mistake protection are affected by earlier choices.
8. **Performance Review** — XP, Trust, Credits, skill categories, decision consequences, stars, and promotion.

## Core systems

- **XP:** promotion progress
- **Trust:** relationships and decision quality
- **Credits:** future upgrades and cosmetics
- **Stars:** shift rating only

## Skill tracks

- Professionalism
- People Skills
- Problem Solving
- Operations

Each encounter also records specific Workplace Readiness Skills for later teacher reporting.

## Replay differences

Choices can add or remove time, create message noise, unlock phishing hints, add ally protection, create route penalties, and change the promotion outcome.

## Not included in this milestone

- authentication
- teacher dashboard
- multiplayer
- AI grading
- paid systems
